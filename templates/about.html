About CuttyUrl

CuttyUrl is a free and easy-to-use tool that allows you to shorten long URLs. With CuttyUrl, you can create short, memorable URLs that are easy to share.

CuttyUrl is perfect for:
- Sharing links on social media
- Sending links in emails
- Including links in your website or blog

CuttyUr is also a great way to track clicks on your links. With CuttyUrl, you can see how many people have clicked on your links and where they are coming from.
To use CuttyUrl, simply enter the long URL in the box and click on the "Shorten URL" button. CuttyUrl will then generate a short, unique URL for you. You can then copy and paste the short URL wherever you want to share it.
CuttyUrl is a great way to make your links more shareable and trackable. Sign up today and start shortening your links!
